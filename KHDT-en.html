CAREER INFORMATION

JOB OBJECTIVE

  • Prepare target plans, consolidate progress, and analyze financial efficiency for real estate investment projects.
  • Advise management on feasibility assessment and appraisal of new investment opportunities.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Planning: Coordinate with departments to prepare target plans, schedules, and project cash flow plans.
  • Financial analysis: Build financial models and calculate NPV/IRR to evaluate project efficiency.
  • Monitoring: Monitor project plan execution, report deviations, and propose solutions.
  • Appraisal: Research markets, review legal documents, and assess partner capacity in M&A transactions.
  • Reporting: Ensure the quality of periodic investment management reports submitted to the Board of Management.

AUTHORITY

  • Proactively perform duties under approved investment plans.
  • Request relevant departments to provide documents needed for project appraisal.
  • Receive advanced training in finance and real estate project management.

QUALIFICATIONS

Education: Bachelor degree or above in Economics, Finance, Law, Management, or related fields.

Experience: At least 2 years of experience in investment planning at real estate groups.

Professional skills:

  • Proficient in project financial modeling.
  • Strong analytical, synthesis, and reporting skills.
  • Good understanding of investment and housing regulations.

Personal qualities: Integrity, logical thinking, and ability to work under pressure.
